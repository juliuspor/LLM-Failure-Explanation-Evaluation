The bug occurs because the code unconditionally accesses indices 3 and 4 after checking length != 2, but for a length of 3 or 4, those indices are invalid. The fix moves the validation of characters at indices 3 and 4 inside the specific branches where the length is known to be at least 5 (for the 5-character case) or at least 7 (for the longer case). This ensures index safety and maintains the original validation logic.
