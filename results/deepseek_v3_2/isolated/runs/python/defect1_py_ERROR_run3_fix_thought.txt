The bug was in the calculation of total minutes. The original code incorrectly handled the sign of minutes_offset when hours_offset was negative. It attempted to adjust minutes_offset based on the sign of hours_in_minutes, which could lead to incorrect total minutes. The fix simplifies the calculation: total_minutes = hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset. This directly combines the hours and minutes components, preserving the correct sign for both, and then multiplies by MILLIS_PER_MINUTE to get the offset in milliseconds.
