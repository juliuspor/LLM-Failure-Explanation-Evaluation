The bug diagnosis incorrectly identifies the issue; the actual bug is in the `_update_bounds` method where `self._max_middle_index` incorrectly references `self._min_middle_index` when fetching the period for comparison. This causes the max middle index to be compared against the min middle period, leading to incorrect bounds. The fix is to change line referencing `self._min_middle_index` to `self._max_middle_index` when obtaining `max_middle_period`.
