The bug occurs because when hours_offset is negative, the calculation incorrectly modifies minutes_offset to become negative, which then fails the range check. The fix is to compute total minutes correctly by adding hours_offset * 60 to minutes_offset, preserving the sign of hours_offset, and then validate the total minutes within -23*60 to +23*60. Also adjust the validation to allow minutes_offset 0-59 regardless of sign, as the sign is handled by hours_offset.
