The bug was that after each replacement, text_index and replace_index were not reset to -1 before searching for the next match. This caused the algorithm to incorrectly compare new matches with the previous match's index, potentially skipping earlier matches. The fix is to reset text_index and replace_index to -1 after updating start, ensuring the next search starts fresh. Also, the increase variable is unused but harmless; we leave it as is to maintain compatibility.
