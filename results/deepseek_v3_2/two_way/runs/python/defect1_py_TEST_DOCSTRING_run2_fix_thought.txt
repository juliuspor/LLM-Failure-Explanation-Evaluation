The bug was in the sign handling for minutes when hours are negative. The original code incorrectly adjusted minutes_offset based on the sign of hours_offset, leading to wrong total minutes. The fix ensures that minutes_offset is treated as an absolute magnitude when hours are negative, as per the documented behavior. Now, for (-2, 15), total_minutes becomes -2*60 + 15 = -105 minutes, which correctly yields offset -6300000 ms and ID "-02:15". The validation also updated to allow minutes_offset from -59 to 59 to support mixed signs.
