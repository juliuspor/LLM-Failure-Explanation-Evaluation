The bug is in the `_update_bounds` method when updating `_max_middle_index`. It incorrectly uses `self._min_middle_index` to fetch the period for comparison, instead of `self._max_middle_index`. This leads to comparing the new middle with the wrong existing middle value, causing incorrect index assignment. The fix is to replace `self._min_middle_index` with `self._max_middle_index` when fetching `max_middle_period`.
