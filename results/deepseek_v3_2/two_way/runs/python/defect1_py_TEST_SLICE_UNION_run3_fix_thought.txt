The bug is in the sign handling when hours are negative and minutes are positive. The current code subtracts minutes from hours_in_minutes, which can result in a positive total when hours_in_minutes is negative and minutes is positive. Instead, we should compute total minutes as hours_in_minutes + minutes_offset, where minutes_offset carries the sign of the original minutes (which is always positive per validation). So total_minutes = hours_in_minutes + minutes_offset, and then convert to milliseconds. This ensures that negative hours and positive minutes combine correctly to a negative offset.
