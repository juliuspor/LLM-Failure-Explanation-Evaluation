The bug is in the `_update_bounds` method where `_max_middle_index` incorrectly references `_min_middle_index` when retrieving the period for comparison. This causes the wrong period to be used for max middle comparison, leading to incorrect index updates. The fix is to change `self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index)` to `self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index)` on line 379.
