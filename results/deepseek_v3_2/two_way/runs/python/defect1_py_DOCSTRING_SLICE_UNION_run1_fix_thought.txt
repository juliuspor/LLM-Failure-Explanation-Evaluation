The bug was in the calculation of the total offset in minutes when the hours_offset is negative. The code incorrectly subtracted minutes_offset from the negative hours_in_minutes, making the offset more negative than intended. For example, with hours_offset=-1 and minutes_offset=30, it produced -90 minutes instead of -30. The fix is to always add minutes_offset to hours_in_minutes, regardless of sign, because minutes_offset is already a positive value representing the minutes component.
