The bug is in the `_update_bounds` method where the `_max_middle_index` update incorrectly uses `self._min_middle_index` to fetch the period for comparison. Specifically, line 383 uses `self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index).get_period()` instead of `self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index).get_period()`. This causes the max middle index to be compared against the min middle period, leading to incorrect updates. The fix is to replace `self._min_middle_index` with `self._max_middle_index` in that section.
