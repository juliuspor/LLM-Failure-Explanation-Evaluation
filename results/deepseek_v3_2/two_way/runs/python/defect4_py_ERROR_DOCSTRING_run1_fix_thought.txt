The bug was in the max_middle_index update logic: it incorrectly referenced self._min_middle_index when fetching the period to compare against, instead of self._max_middle_index. This caused the wrong middle timestamp to be used for comparison, leading to an incorrect max_middle_index. The fix changes the line `max_middle_period = self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index).get_period()` to `max_middle_period = self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index).get_period()`.
