The bug was that the function incorrectly validated minutes_offset as 0-59, but the minutes component can be negative (e.g., -15) to represent offsets like +05:-15. The fix expands the validation to allow minutes_offset between -59 and 59, and also validates the total offset in minutes to ensure it's within -23:59 to +23:59. The calculation of total_minutes is simplified to directly combine hours and minutes, removing the previous buggy logic that incorrectly adjusted minutes_offset based on the sign of hours_offset.
