The bug was in the max middle index update logic: it incorrectly referenced `self._min_middle_index` when fetching the period for comparison, instead of `self._max_middle_index`. This caused the wrong period to be used for calculating the current max middle value, leading to incorrect index assignments. The fix changes `self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index)` to `self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index)` in that section.
