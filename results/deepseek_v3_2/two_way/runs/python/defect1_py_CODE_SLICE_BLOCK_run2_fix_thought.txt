The bug is that the variable `minutes_offset` is being reused to store the total offset in minutes, which can be outside the 0-59 range and also loses the original minutes value. The validation for minutes being 0-59 is already done by the caller or the offset parsing. The fix is to compute the total offset in minutes without overwriting the original `minutes_offset` parameter, and then convert to milliseconds. We'll rename the local variable to avoid confusion and ensure the calculation is correct for both positive and negative hours.
