The bug is in line 383 where negative hours_offset leads to an incorrect total minutes calculation. For negative hours_offset, we should also add minutes_offset (since minutes_offset is a positive component of the offset). The fix is to change the subtraction to addition for negative offsets, or more simply, always compute total minutes as hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset, adjusting for sign. The simplest fix is to compute total minutes directly: hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset, which works for both positive and negative hours_offset because minutes_offset is always positive (0-59).
