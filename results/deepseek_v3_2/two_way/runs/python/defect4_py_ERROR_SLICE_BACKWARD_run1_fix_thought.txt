The bug is in the `_update_bounds` method where `_max_middle_index` is incorrectly computed using `self._min_middle_index` instead of `self._max_middle_index`. This causes the max middle index to be based on the wrong period, leading to incorrect bounds. The fix is to replace `self._min_middle_index` with `self._max_middle_index` when fetching the period for max middle comparison. Additionally, ensure that when start/middle/end values are equal, the index is not updated to maintain consistency (first encountered remains).
