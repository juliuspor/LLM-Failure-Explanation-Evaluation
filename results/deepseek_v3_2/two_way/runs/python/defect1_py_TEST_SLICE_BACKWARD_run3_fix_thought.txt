The bug was that when hours_offset is negative and minutes_offset is positive, the code incorrectly combined them by adding the minutes to the hours without adjusting the sign. Specifically, lines 378-381 attempted to adjust minutes_offset based on hours_offset, but the logic was flawed. The fix is to compute total minutes as hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset, which automatically handles the sign because hours_offset is negative. Then convert total minutes to milliseconds. This ensures the correct offset and ID.
