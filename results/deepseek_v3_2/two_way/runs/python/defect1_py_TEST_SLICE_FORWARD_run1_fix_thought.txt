The bug is in the calculation of total minutes when hours and minutes have opposite signs. The original code incorrectly subtracts minutes from hours when they have opposite signs, but the correct approach is to add the minutes (with their sign) to the hours converted to minutes. Since minutes already has its sign, we should simply add hours_in_minutes and minutes_offset to get the total minutes, then multiply by MILLIS_PER_MINUTE. The fix is to change line 383 from `total_minutes = hours_in_minutes - minutes_offset` to `total_minutes = hours_in_minutes + minutes_offset`.
