The bug is in the calculation of total minutes when hours_offset is negative. Currently, it subtracts minutes_offset from hours_in_minutes, which is incorrect because both are negative. The correct approach is to add minutes_offset to hours_in_minutes (since hours_in_minutes is negative, adding a positive minutes_offset yields the correct total negative offset). For consistency, we should treat both positive and negative hours_offset the same: total_minutes = hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset. This works because minutes_offset is always positive (0-59).
