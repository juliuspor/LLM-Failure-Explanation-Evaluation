The bug was in the calculation of total minutes when hours_offset is negative. The original code incorrectly assigned minutes_offset to total_minutes after checking sign, which could lead to wrong sign for minutes. The fix ensures that when hours_offset is negative, minutes_offset is subtracted from the negative total minutes, preserving the correct negative offset. For positive hours, minutes are added as before. This yields the correct total minutes, e.g., (-2, 15) -> -2*60 - 15 = -135 minutes, which matches the expected offset -02:15.
