The bug is that minutes_offset is validated only for being between 0 and 59, but it should also be consistent with the sign of hours_offset. For example, hours_offset=-5 and minutes_offset=30 would produce a total offset of -4.5 hours, which is not a standard offset representation. The fix is to ensure that minutes_offset has the same sign as hours_offset when hours_offset is non-zero, or is zero when hours_offset is zero. Additionally, we should validate that the total offset is within the valid range of -24 to +24 hours (or -86400000 to +86400000 milliseconds).
