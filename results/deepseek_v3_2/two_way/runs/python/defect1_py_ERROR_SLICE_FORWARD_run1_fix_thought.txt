The bug was in the calculation of total minutes. The original code incorrectly handled the sign of minutes_offset by conditionally subtracting or adding, which could result in negative minutes_offset being passed to safe_multiply. The fix simplifies the calculation: total_minutes = hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset. This correctly combines the hours and minutes components, preserving the sign from hours_offset and adding the minutes_offset (which is already validated to be between 0 and 59). This ensures total_minutes can be negative, zero, or positive, and is then multiplied by MILLIS_PER_MINUTE to get the offset in milliseconds. The validation for minutes_offset remains (0-59) as minutes_offset is the magnitude of the minutes component, not the total offset sign.
