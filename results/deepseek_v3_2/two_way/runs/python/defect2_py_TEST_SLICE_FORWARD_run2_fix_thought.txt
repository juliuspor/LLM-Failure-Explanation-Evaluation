The bug is that the get_paint method uses the original 'value' parameter in the interpolation formula instead of the clamped value 'v'. After clamping, we should compute 'g' based on 'v', not 'value'. This ensures that values outside the bounds are clamped to the bounds and then mapped to the appropriate gray shade. The fix is to replace 'value' with 'v' in the calculation of 'g'.
