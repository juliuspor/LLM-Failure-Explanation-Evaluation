The issue is that when `start` and `end` are equal, the gap becomes zero, leading to `_next_int_java(rnd, 0)` which raises a ValueError with 'bound must be positive'. However, the test expects a message containing 'start' and 'end'. The fix is to add a check in `_random_internal` for a zero gap when `chars` is None, and raise a ValueError with a message that includes 'start' and 'end'.
