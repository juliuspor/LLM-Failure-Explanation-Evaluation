The bug is that `_max_middle_index` incorrectly uses `_min_middle_index` to fetch the period for comparison, causing the wrong middle value to be compared. In the block for `_max_middle_index`, line 379 uses `self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index).get_period()` instead of `self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index).get_period()`. This means the max middle comparison is always against the min middle period, leading to incorrect updates. The fix is to replace `_min_middle_index` with `_max_middle_index` in that block.
