The bug is in the offset calculation logic for mixed signs (negative hours, positive minutes). The current code incorrectly sets minutes_offset to negative when hours are negative, but it should keep the sign of minutes separate. The correct approach is to compute total minutes as hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset, then convert to milliseconds. The validation for minutes_offset should allow 0-59 regardless of hours sign, as the test expects -2 hours and 15 minutes to produce offset -02:15.
