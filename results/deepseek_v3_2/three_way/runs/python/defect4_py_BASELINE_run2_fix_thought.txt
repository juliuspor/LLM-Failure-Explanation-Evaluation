The bug is a copy-paste error where `self._min_middle_index` is used instead of `self._max_middle_index` when retrieving the period for the current maximum middle. This causes incorrect comparison and assignment of `self._max_middle_index`. The fix is to replace `self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index)` with `self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index)` on line 373.
