The bug was in the validation and offset calculation. The validation incorrectly restricted minutes_offset to 0-59, but minutes can be negative (e.g., -15) when hours are positive, and positive when hours are negative. The offset calculation also mishandled sign by adjusting minutes_offset incorrectly. The fix: 1) Allow minutes_offset from -59 to 59. 2) Compute total_minutes directly as hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset, which correctly combines signs. 3) Use total_minutes in the multiplication to get millis offset. This ensures (-2, 15) yields -02:15 and (2, -15) raises ValueError as expected.
