The bug occurs because the `translate` method uses `pos` to index into `code_units` without ensuring it's within bounds after incrementing by `len(c)`. When a surrogate pair is processed, `len(c)` is 2, but `pos` may already be at the last valid index, leading to an out-of-range access in the next loop iteration. The fix is to adjust the loop condition to `while pos < length` and ensure `pos` is incremented correctly when `consumed == 0` and a surrogate pair is written.
