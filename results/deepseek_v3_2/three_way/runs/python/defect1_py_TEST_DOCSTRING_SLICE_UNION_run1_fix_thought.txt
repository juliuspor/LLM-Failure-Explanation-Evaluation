The bug was that minutes_offset validation incorrectly restricted to 0-59, but minutes_offset should be allowed to be negative (e.g., -15) to represent offsets like -02:15. Also, the calculation of total minutes was flawed: it tried to adjust minutes_offset based on the sign of hours_offset, which is unnecessary. The fix is to change the validation range for minutes_offset to -59..59 and compute total_minutes as hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset directly. This handles both positive and negative minutes correctly.
