The bug is in the `_update_bounds` method where the maximum middle index update incorrectly uses `self._min_middle_index` to fetch the comparison period. This leads to incorrect tracking of the maximum middle index. The fix is to change line 370 from `max_middle_period = self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index).get_period()` to `max_middle_period = self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index).get_period()`. This ensures the comparison is made against the current maximum middle period, not the minimum middle period.
