The issue is that the method should validate that hours and minutes have the same sign (or minutes can be zero) when hours is non-zero. If hours is positive, minutes must be >=0; if hours is negative, minutes must be <=0. This ensures offset consistency. The validation was missing, causing (2, -15) to not raise ValueError. Also, the offset calculation for negative hours with positive minutes was incorrect because it didn't enforce sign consistency. The fix adds sign validation and corrects the offset calculation to combine hours and minutes with proper sign handling.
