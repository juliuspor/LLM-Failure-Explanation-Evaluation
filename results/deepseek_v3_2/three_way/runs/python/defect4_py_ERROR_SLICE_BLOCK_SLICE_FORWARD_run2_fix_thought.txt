The bug is in the _update_bounds method where the max_middle_index update incorrectly references self._min_middle_index instead of self._max_middle_index when fetching the period for comparison. This causes the max_middle_index to be compared against the wrong period, leading to incorrect index assignments. Also, the min/max start/end indices are updated correctly, but the middle indices have a typo. Fix by using self._max_middle_index to get the correct period for max_middle comparison.
