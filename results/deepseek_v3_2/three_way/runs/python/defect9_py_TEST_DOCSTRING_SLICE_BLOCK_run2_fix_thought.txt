The bug occurs when `replacement_list[i]` is `None` because we call `len(replacement_list[i])` without checking for `None`. The fix is to skip the length calculation for `None` replacements, as they are already handled earlier in the loop (where `replacement_list[i] is None` leads to skipping). We should move the length calculation inside a condition that ensures both `replacement_list[i]` and `search_list[i]` are not `None` and have positive length.
