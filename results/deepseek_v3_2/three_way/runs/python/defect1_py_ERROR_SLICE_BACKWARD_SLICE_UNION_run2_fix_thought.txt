The bug was that the validation for minutes_offset only allowed positive values (0-59), but minutes_offset can be negative when the hours_offset is negative. The fix is to allow minutes_offset in the range -59 to 59. Also, the calculation of total minutes was incorrect: it was reassigning minutes_offset incorrectly. Instead, compute total_minutes directly as hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset. This handles both positive and negative minutes correctly.
