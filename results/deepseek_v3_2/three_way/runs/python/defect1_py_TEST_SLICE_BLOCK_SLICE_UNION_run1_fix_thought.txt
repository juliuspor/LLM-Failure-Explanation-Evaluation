The bug was in the calculation of total minutes. The original code incorrectly adjusted minutes_offset based on the sign of hours_offset, which could lead to wrong offsets when hours and minutes have different signs. The fix simplifies the calculation: total_minutes = hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset. This correctly handles mixed signs (e.g., -2 hours and +15 minutes yields -120 + 15 = -105 minutes). The validation remains the same, as minutes_offset must be between 0 and 59 regardless of sign, which is correct because minutes offset is a magnitude, not a signed value. The sign is determined by hours_offset.
