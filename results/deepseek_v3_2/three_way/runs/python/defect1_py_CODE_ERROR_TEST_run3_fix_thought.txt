The bug was that the validation for minutes_offset incorrectly rejected negative minutes even when hours were negative. The fix is to allow minutes_offset in the range -59 to 59, but enforce that the sign of minutes_offset matches the sign of hours_offset: when hours are positive, minutes must be non-negative; when hours are negative, minutes must be non-positive. This ensures that the total offset is correctly computed and matches the expected format like '-02:15'.
