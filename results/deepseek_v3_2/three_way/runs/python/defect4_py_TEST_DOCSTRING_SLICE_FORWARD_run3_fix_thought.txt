The bug was in the `_max_middle_index` update logic where it incorrectly referenced `self._min_middle_index` instead of `self._max_middle_index` when fetching the period for comparison. This caused the wrong middle timestamp to be used for maximum comparison. Fixed by changing `self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index)` to `self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index)` in the max middle calculation block.
