The bug is a typo in the `_update_bounds` method where `self._min_middle_index` is used instead of `self._max_middle_index` when fetching the period for max middle comparison. This causes the max middle index to be incorrectly updated. The fix is to change line 373 from `self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index).get_period()` to `self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index).get_period()`. The rest of the method remains unchanged.
