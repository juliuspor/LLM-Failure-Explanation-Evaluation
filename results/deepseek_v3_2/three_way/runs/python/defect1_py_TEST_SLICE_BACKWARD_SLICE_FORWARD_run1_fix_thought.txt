The bug is in the calculation of total minutes when hours are negative and minutes are positive. The current logic incorrectly combines hours and minutes when hours are negative. The fix is to compute total minutes as hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset, preserving the sign of hours and minutes. This ensures that negative hours and positive minutes are correctly combined (e.g., -2 hours + 15 minutes = -120 + 15 = -105 minutes, which is the correct offset for -02:15).
