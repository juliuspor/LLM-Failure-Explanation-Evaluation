The bug was that the validation for minutes_offset incorrectly assumed it must be non-negative (0-59). However, minutes_offset can be negative when the total offset is negative (e.g., -02:15). The fix is to allow minutes_offset in the range -59 to 59, and then compute total_minutes directly as hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset, which correctly handles both positive and negative offsets. This also simplifies the logic by removing the conditional adjustment of minutes_offset.
