The bug is in the sign handling when combining hours and minutes. The original code incorrectly modifies the minutes_offset variable based on the sign of hours_in_minutes, but the logic is flawed for negative hours and positive minutes. The correct approach is to compute total minutes as hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset, preserving the sign of hours_offset, then convert to milliseconds. The validation should allow minutes_offset from 0 to 59 regardless of hours sign.
