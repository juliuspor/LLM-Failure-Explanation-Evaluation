The bug is that minutes_offset validation incorrectly rejects negative values. The docstring and test suite indicate that negative minutes should be allowed when hours_offset is negative, to represent offsets like -02:15. The fix is to change the validation range from (0, 59) to (-59, 59). This allows negative minutes for negative hour offsets, and also ensures minutes magnitude doesn't exceed 59. The rest of the method already handles the sign correctly: when hours_in_minutes is negative, it subtracts minutes_offset (which could be negative, resulting in addition of magnitude).
