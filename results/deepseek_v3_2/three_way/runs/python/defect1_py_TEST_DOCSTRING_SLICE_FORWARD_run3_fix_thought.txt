The bug was in the calculation of total minutes when hours_offset is negative. The original code incorrectly reassigned minutes_offset, losing the original minutes value and misapplying the sign. The fix introduces a new variable total_minutes to accumulate the total offset in minutes correctly, ensuring that when hours_offset is negative, the minutes_offset is subtracted (making the total more negative), and when positive, minutes_offset is added. This yields the correct millisecond offset and zone ID.
