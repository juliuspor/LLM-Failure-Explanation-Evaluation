The bug was that the variable `minutes_offset` was being reassigned inside the try block, causing confusion and potential errors. The fix introduces a new variable `total_minutes` to hold the sum of hours converted to minutes plus the minutes offset, preserving the original parameter. This ensures clarity and correctness. The validation of hours and minutes remains before the calculation, and the total minutes can be negative (for negative offsets) which is acceptable. The safe_multiply handles overflow.
