The bug is that the validation for minutes_offset incorrectly assumes minutes_offset must be non-negative (0-59). However, the offset can be negative (e.g., -05:30). The fix is to allow minutes_offset in the range -59 to 59, and then compute total minutes by adding hours_offset*60 and minutes_offset directly. The previous code attempted to adjust minutes_offset based on the sign of hours_offset, which was flawed. The new approach simply sums the total minutes and then multiplies by MILLIS_PER_MINUTE.
