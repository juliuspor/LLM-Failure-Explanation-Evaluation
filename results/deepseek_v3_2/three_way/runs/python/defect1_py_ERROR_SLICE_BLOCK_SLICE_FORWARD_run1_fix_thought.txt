The bug was that minutes_offset was being validated after it was potentially modified (by subtracting or adding hours_in_minutes). The fix is to validate minutes_offset input range first, then compute total minutes directly as hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset, which handles both positive and negative minutes correctly. This ensures the total minutes is within -23*60-59 to +23*60+59, and the multiplication to milliseconds will be safe.
