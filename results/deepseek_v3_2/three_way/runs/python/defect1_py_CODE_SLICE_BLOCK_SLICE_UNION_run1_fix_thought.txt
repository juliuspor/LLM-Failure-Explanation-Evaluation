The bug was due to reusing the parameter variable `minutes_offset` for two different purposes: first as the minutes component (0-59), then as the total offset in minutes. This caused confusion and could lead to issues. The fix introduces a new variable `total_minutes` to hold the combined offset in minutes, preserving the original parameter for clarity and correctness. The calculation for negative offsets is also corrected: when `total_minutes` is negative, we subtract the positive minutes component (since minutes_offset is always non-negative) to get a more negative total. This matches the sign handling: e.g., hours_offset=-5, minutes_offset=30 => total_minutes = -300 - 30 = -330. The rest of the logic remains the same.
