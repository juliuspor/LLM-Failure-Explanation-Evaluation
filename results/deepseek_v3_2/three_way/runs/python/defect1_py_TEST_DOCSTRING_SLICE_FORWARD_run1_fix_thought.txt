The bug was in the sign handling for mixed signs. The original code attempted to adjust minutes_offset based on the sign of hours_offset, which was unnecessary and incorrect. The fix simplifies the calculation: total_minutes = hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset. This correctly combines hours and minutes, preserving the sign of hours_offset and adding minutes_offset (which can be positive). This yields the correct total minutes, which is then converted to milliseconds. For example, hours_offset=-2, minutes_offset=15 gives total_minutes = -120 + 15 = -105 minutes, which corresponds to -02:15 offset. The rest of the method remains unchanged.
