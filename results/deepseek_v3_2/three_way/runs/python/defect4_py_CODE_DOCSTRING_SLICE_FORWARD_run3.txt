The code fails because there is a copy-paste error in the `_max_middle_index` update block. On line 45, `max_middle_period = self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index).get_period()` incorrectly uses `self._min_middle_index` instead of `self._max_middle_index`. This means the code compares the new period's middle to the middle of the period at the minimum middle index, not the maximum middle index. As a result, the `_max_middle_index` may not be updated correctly, leading to incorrect tracking of the maximum middle timestamp.
