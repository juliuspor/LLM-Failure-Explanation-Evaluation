### What Happened

### Why It Happened
The logic here is incorrect because, if the `hours_offset` is negative, `minutes_offset` should actually be added, not subtracted. The subtraction flips the sign of `minutes_offset`, leading to incorrect calculation of the total offset in minutes.

### Where It Occurred
