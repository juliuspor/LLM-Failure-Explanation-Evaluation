Why it failed (root cause)
- The method rejects any negative minutes because of the condition minutes_offset < 0.
- You passed minutes = -15 to represent “minus 2 hours and 15 minutes” (total offset -2:15). The code treats negative minutes as invalid instead of combining the sign with hours.
- In short: the code enforces minutes to be in 0..59 and does not accept negative minutes, so (-2, -15) trips the check.
