What went wrong (root cause)
- The code that computes the total offset in minutes misapplies signs when combining hours and minutes. That happens around these lines:
  - line 406: minutes_offset = hours_in_minutes - minutes_offset
  - line 408: minutes_offset = hours_in_minutes + minutes_offset
- Because those assignments reuse the minutes_offset variable and subtract/add in the wrong places, some valid input combinations produce the wrong total minutes (wrong sign or magnitude). That wrong total then gets multiplied by milliseconds and can exceed the allowed range, so the method raises ValueError("Offset is too large") at line 411 even though the requested offset should be valid.
