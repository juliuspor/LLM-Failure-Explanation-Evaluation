      raise ValueError(f"Minutes out of range: {minutes_offset}")

Those combinations are explicitly allowed by the docstring (see the rows "zero -15 -> -00:15" and "-ve -15 -> -02:15"), but the check at lines 400–401 treats negative minutes as invalid and fails immediately.

Why it fails (root cause)
- The validation at lines 400–401 enforces minutes_offset >= 0 unconditionally. The method later (lines 406–409) expects to adjust minutes depending on the hours sign (it has logic to combine minutes with the hour-based minutes). Because the early validation blocks negative minutes, valid negative-minute cases never reach the conversion logic; instead a ValueError is raised at lines 400–401. In short: the validation is too strict and out of sync with the allowed combinations in the docstring.
