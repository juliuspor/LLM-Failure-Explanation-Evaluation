What failed
- In the "max middle" section you compare the new period's middle against the wrong stored period. Instead of using the stored index for the maximum middle (_max_middle_index) you use the stored index for the minimum middle (_min_middle_index):

- That is the root cause: you read the period at _min_middle_index when you intended to read the period at _max_middle_index.

1. Wrong comparisons / wrong result:
