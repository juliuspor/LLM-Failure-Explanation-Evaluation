Why it failed (root cause)
- The code validates minutes_offset too early and rejects negative minute values (minutes_offset < 0). However the parsing/logic earlier produced a negative minutes_offset (−15). The later adjustment code (lines 402–405) clearly intends to accept a negative minutes_offset and combine it with hours_in_minutes to produce the final offset. Because the negative value is rejected at line 396, the later correction never runs and you get the ValueError "Minutes out of range: -15".
- In short: minutes_offset becomes negative (e.g. from parsing a "-01:15" style sign or from arithmetic), but the guard at line 396 forbids negative minutes even though subsequent code was written to handle them.
