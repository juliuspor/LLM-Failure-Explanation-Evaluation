The bug was that translate advanced pos while repeatedly calling code_point_at(code_units, pos) inside a for loop, causing out-of-range calls when surrogate pairs advanced pos by 2. Fix by computing the codepoint once per consumed codepoint iteration and then advancing pos by its char_count. Implemented loop that for each consumed iteration reads cp = code_point_at(code_units, pos) then pos += char_count(cp). Preserve other behavior and validations.
