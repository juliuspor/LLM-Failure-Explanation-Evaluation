Why it happened (concise cause)
- The parser conflates two different valid forms:
  - language_country (e.g. "en_US") — country in positions 3–4
  - language__variant (e.g. "fr__POSIX") — empty country, variant starts at position 4
- The code only implements the first form’s validation and rejects the second because it forces uppercase letters at index 3.
