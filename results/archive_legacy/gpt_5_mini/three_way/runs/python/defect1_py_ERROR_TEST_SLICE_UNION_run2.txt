What happened (what failed)

- The method raises ValueError: "Minutes out of range: -15".
- That ValueError is raised at the guard on line 396–397:

      raise ValueError(f"Minutes out of range: {minutes_offset}")

- Later code (lines 402 and 404) expects to be able to adjust minutes_offset for negative-hour cases, but that code is never reached because the early check already raised for negative minutes.
- In short: the range check is too strict/wrong. It enforces minutes in [0, 59] rather than the intended range (which permits negative minutes in some cases, or at least permits minutes in [-59, 59]).

Where exactly in the code
- The failing check is at lines 396–397 in the shown slice.
- The adjustment logic that would handle minute sign is at lines 402 and 404 but is unreachable when minutes_offset is negative because of the earlier raise.
