- Why it fails: mantissa is never changed inside that while loop. You test mantissa % 10 == 0 (line 181) and compute check_val from mantissa, but you never assign a new value to mantissa (no mantissa = ... or mantissa //= ...). If mantissa is divisible by 10 the condition mantissa % 10 == 0 stays true forever, so the loop never terminates (infinite loop).
