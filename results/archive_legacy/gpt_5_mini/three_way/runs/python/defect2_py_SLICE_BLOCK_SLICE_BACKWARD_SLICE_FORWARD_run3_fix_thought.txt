The bug is that get_paint computes g from the original value variable instead of the clamped v, which allows g to fall outside 0-255; also it should guard against division by zero (though __init__ already prevents equal bounds) and should clamp/round the computed g and provide a clear error if needed. Fix: use clamped v, compute ratio, round and clamp g into [0,255], and raise a ValueError if bounds are invalid (defensive).
