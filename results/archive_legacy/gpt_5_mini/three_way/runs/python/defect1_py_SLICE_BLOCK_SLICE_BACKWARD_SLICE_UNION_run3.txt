Why it fails (root cause)
- minutes_offset is being used both as a magnitude (0..59) and as a signed value (negative to mean subtract minutes). - In short: the code first forbids negative minutes (raising "Minutes out of range"), then later assumes negative minutes are valid and tries to use them; those two assumptions conflict and the check is incorrect for signed offsets.
