Why it happens (root cause)
- The minutes validation is too strict: it assumes minutes must always be non‑negative. The correct rule is more nuanced:
  - minutes_offset must be in the range -59 .. +59 (so negative minutes are allowed).
  - However, a negative minutes value is not allowed when hours_offset is positive (example: (2, -15) should be invalid).
  - Other combinations in the docstring are valid: hours > 0 with minutes >= 0; hours == 0 with minutes positive or negative; hours < 0 with minutes positive or negative.
- The current check enforces minutes_offset >= 0 for all hours, which is why valid inputs like (0, -15) or (-2, -15) fail at lines 396–397.

   - After the range check, add a check that disallows negative minutes only when hours_offset is positive:
