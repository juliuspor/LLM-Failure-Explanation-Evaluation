Why it fails (root cause)

  - Later, at lines 402 and 404 the code manipulates minutes_offset with expressions that assume minutes_offset can be negative (minutes_offset = hours_in_minutes - minutes_offset and minutes_offset = hours_in_minutes + minutes_offset).

- In short: the validation happens before (and contradicts) the sign/normalization logic. The code both forbids negative minutes and then expects negative minutes to be possible later — that's the direct cause of the error.
