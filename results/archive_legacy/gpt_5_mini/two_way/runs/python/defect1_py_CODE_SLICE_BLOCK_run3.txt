- The ValueError is raised at the check on lines 396–397:

      raise ValueError(f"Minutes out of range: {minutes_offset}")
- That check rejects negative minute values. If a caller passes a negative minutes_offset (for example to represent −1:30 as hours_offset = -1 and minutes_offset = -30) this validation fails and the method throws the ValueError immediately.

Why it fails (root cause)
- The method mixes two different conventions for the minutes parameter:

  - The validation at lines 396–397, however, forbids negative minutes, so any caller that passes negative minutes (a reasonable representation for negative offsets) will be rejected before combination.
- In short: the input validation on minutes_offset is inconsistent with the intended use of minutes_offset, so valid callers that provide negative minute offsets are incorrectly rejected.
