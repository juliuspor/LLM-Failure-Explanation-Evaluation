The to_locale method currently returns a 2-part Locale before validating the underscore at index 2 and exact length, allowing malformed strings. I will rewrite the method to strictly validate formats: accept exactly length 2 (language), exactly length 5 with underscore at pos2 and uppercase country, or length >=7 with underscores at pos2 and pos5 and proper case checks. Raise ValueError for invalid formats. Handle None input. Return Locale objects accordingly.
