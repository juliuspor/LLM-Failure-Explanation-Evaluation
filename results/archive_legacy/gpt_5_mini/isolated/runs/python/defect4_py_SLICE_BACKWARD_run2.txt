If min_start_period is NOT a SimpleTimePeriod, min_start is never assigned. That causes an UnboundLocalError (local variable 'min_start' referenced before assignment) at line 346.

Because of wrong indentation or a stray statement, the code always assigns index to _min_start_index even when the comparison (line 346) should decide whether to update it. That makes the comparison pointless and will incorrectly change _min_start_index for every item.
