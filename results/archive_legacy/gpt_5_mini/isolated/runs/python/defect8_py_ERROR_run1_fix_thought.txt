The original to_locale rejected locale strings like 'fr__POSIX' because it assumed the country must be two uppercase letters at positions 3-4. The fix is to allow an empty country when a variant exists (double underscore), and to parse by splitting on underscores with a maxsplit of 2, validating each part appropriately. Preserve original strict checks for language (2 lowercase letters) and country (either empty or 2 uppercase letters). Return Locale objects accordingly or raise ValueError for invalid formats.
