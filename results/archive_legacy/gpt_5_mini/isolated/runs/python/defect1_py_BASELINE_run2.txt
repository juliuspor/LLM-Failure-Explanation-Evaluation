What happened (where it failed)
- The failure comes from the validation at lines 396–397:

      raise ValueError(f"Minutes out of range: {minutes_offset}")
- This rejects any negative minutes (minutes_offset < 0), so a legitimate call like DateTimeZone.for_offset_hours_minutes(0, -15) or (-2, -15) triggers ValueError: Minutes out of range: -15 even though the docstring allows minutes in the range -59..+59.

Why it failed (root cause)
- The code enforces the wrong minutes range. The docstring and the tests expect minutes to be allowed from -59 to +59; the code mistakenly allows only 0..59.
- Because the check runs before any sign/combining logic, the call fails immediately at that validation and never reaches the arithmetic below.
