What happened (where it fails)
- The code rejects any negative minutes immediately at line 400–401:
  - 400: if minutes_offset < 0 or minutes_offset > 59:
  - 401:     raise ValueError(f"Minutes out of range: {minutes_offset}")
- That means calls that should be allowed by the docstring — for example (0, -15) or (-2, -15) — throw a ValueError at line 401 and never reach the later logic that combines hour and minute signs (lines 406–409).
- The later code (lines 406–408) expects minutes_offset can be negative in some cases and adjusts minutes_offset by adding/subtracting hours_in_minutes, so the early rejection is the root cause.

Why it fails (root cause)
- The validation at line 400 is too strict. It treats any negative minutes as out-of-range even though the docstring explicitly allows negative minutes when hours are zero or negative.
- As a result, legitimate offsets like:
  - (0, -15) → +00:15? actually should be -00:15 (doc allows zero hour + negative minutes → -00:15)
  - (-2, -15) → -02:15 (doc allows negative hour + negative minute → -02:15)
  are incorrectly rejected before the sign-merging logic runs.

Exactly where to change it
- Replace the check at line 400 with a conditional that only rejects negative minutes when they are disallowed by the hours sign. Concretely:
  - Keep the upper bound check minutes_offset > 59.
  - Only reject minutes_offset < 0 when hours_offset > 0 (positive hour and negative minutes is invalid per doc).
  - Ensure minutes_offset >= -59 overall.

Suggested patch (precise change)
- Change:
    if minutes_offset < 0 or minutes_offset > 59:
        raise ValueError(f"Minutes out of range: {minutes_offset}")
- To:
    if minutes_offset > 59 or minutes_offset < -59:
        raise ValueError(f"Minutes out of range: {minutes_offset}")
    if minutes_offset < 0 and hours_offset > 0:
        raise ValueError("Positive hours with negative minutes is invalid")

Summary
- The immediate rejection of any negative minutes at lines 400–401 contradicts the documented allowed combinations and prevents legitimate offsets from being constructed. Adjust the validation so negative minutes are only disallowed when hours are positive, and allow minutes down to -59.
