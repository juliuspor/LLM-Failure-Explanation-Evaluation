Why it fails (root cause)
- The method assumes minutes_offset must be in the range 0..59 only:
  if minutes_offset < 0 or minutes_offset > 59:
      raise ValueError(f"Minutes out of range: {minutes_offset}")
- That check does not allow a negative minutes value even when the caller intends a negative total offset by giving a negative hours (-2) and negative minutes (-15).
- The logic never normalizes the sign across hours and minutes. The code treats minutes_offset as independently signed, and rejects negative minutes instead of combining -2 hours and -15 minutes into a single offset of -135 minutes (i.e. "-02:15").
