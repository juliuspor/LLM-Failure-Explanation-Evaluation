- The ValueError you saw ("Minutes out of range: -15") is produced by the validation that runs when the method receives a negative minutes argument. Why it failed (root cause)
- The API treats the sign of the offset as coming from the hours parameter (or requires minutes to be non-negative). Your call passed hours = -2 and minutes = -15, but the code validates minutes as a standalone value and flags -15 as invalid.
- In other words, the code does not accept negative minutes values. It expects minutes to be in the range 0..59 and applies the sign from hours when combining them. Because you passed a negative minutes (-15), validation fails immediately and you get the error.
