The original to_locale incorrectly assumed positions 3 and 4 are always uppercase letters, failing on strings with an empty country (double underscore), e.g., 'fr__POSIX'. The fix explicitly handles the double-underscore empty-country case, validating language, verifying separators, validating country when present, and extracting the variant at the correct index. The logic accepts lengths 2, 5, or >=7 and raises ValueError for invalid formats. Returning Locale instances with empty parts as needed.
