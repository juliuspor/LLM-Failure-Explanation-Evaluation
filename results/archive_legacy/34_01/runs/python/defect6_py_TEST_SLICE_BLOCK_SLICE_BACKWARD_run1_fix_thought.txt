The bug arises because add_number may call self.add separately for sign and digits, which interacts badly with is_word_char incorrectly classifying '-' as word char. To avoid dependence on add() spacing behavior, construct the full numeric token (including sign when needed) and call self.add once. Preserve original formatting behavior: when x is integer-valued (and not inf/nan), represent as integer with optional mantissa/exp shortening; otherwise use str(abs_x) and prefix sign for negatives and negative zero. Use is_negative_zero to detect -0.0.
