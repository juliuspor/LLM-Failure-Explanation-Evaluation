What happened (where it fails)
- At lines 400–401 the code rejects any negative minutes:
  400 |         if minutes_offset < 0 or minutes_offset > 59:
  401 |             raise ValueError(f"Minutes out of range: {minutes_offset}")
- That unconditional check prevents valid inputs described in the docstring — for example (0, -15) and (-2, -15) are documented as allowed — so calls with negative minutes fail even though they should succeed.

Why it fails (root cause)
- The docstring allows negative minutes when hours_offset is zero or negative. The code, however, treats any negative minutes as invalid. In short: the minutes validation is too strict; it does not consider the sign of hours_offset before rejecting negative minutes.

Exact place to fix
- Modify the conditional at line 400 so it only rejects negative minutes when hours_offset is positive. Keep the upper bound check (> 59) as-is.
