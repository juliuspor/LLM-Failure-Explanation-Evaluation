Why it fails (exact cause)

  This is wrong when minutes is already negative (as in your test). For (-2, -15) that computes -120 + -(-15) = -105 minutes → -01:45, not -135 minutes → -02:15.
- In short: the code re-applies a sign to minutes instead of respecting the minutes argument’s sign. That produces an incorrect combined offset or triggers a validation failure.
