2. self._upper_bound may equal self._lower_bound. If those are equal the division (upper - lower) is zero and your expression will raise ZeroDivisionError at line 73. 3. The error message itself is misleading: the f-string contains no variable values, so it gives "Color parameter outside of expected range: Red Green Blue" instead of showing which value(s) actually caused the problem. That hides useful diagnostic information.
