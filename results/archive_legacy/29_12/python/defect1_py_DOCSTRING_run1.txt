- Cause B — Incorrect magnitude/overflow check:

    if not (-23 <= hours_offset <= 23):
        raise ValueError("hours_offset must be in -23..23")
    if not (-59 <= minutes_offset <= 59):
        raise ValueError("minutes_offset must be in -59..59")
    # Disallow positive hours with negative minutes (docstring rule)

        raise ValueError("minutes cannot be negative when hours are positive")

Why this fixes the two causes
- It does range checks on hours and minutes separately (so you get clear errors if the raw inputs are out of spec).  
- It enforces the docstring rule that hours > 0 and minutes < 0 is invalid.  
- It computes total_seconds with sign preserved (so (-2, -15) becomes -8100 seconds) and then checks the absolute value against the allowed maximum. That avoids false overflow detection caused by mixing up signs or using abs() on components instead of on the resulting offset.
