The original function has two bugs: 1) It incorrectly handles the sign when combining hours and minutes - when hours_offset is negative, it subtracts minutes instead of adding them (e.g., -5 hours + 30 minutes should be -330 total minutes, not -360). 2) The validation for minutes_offset is incorrect - it only validates 0-59 but doesn't account for combined hours+minutes exceeding the valid range. The fix ensures proper addition of hours and minutes regardless of sign, and validates that the total minutes stays within ±24 hours (±1440 minutes).
