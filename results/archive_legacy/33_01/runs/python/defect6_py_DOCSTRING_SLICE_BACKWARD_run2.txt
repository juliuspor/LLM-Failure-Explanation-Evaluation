Why it failed (root cause)
- Your object has an append method that delegates to add (or append is overridden elsewhere to call add). That means:
  - add calls self.append(...)
  - self.append calls add(...)
  - add calls self.append(...)
  - ...and so on forever.
- In short: add and append call each other recursively, producing infinite recursion (maximum recursion depth exceeded).

How I know this is the cause
- The traceback slice you showed points from add (line 126) to a call to self.append (line 138). That pattern is the canonical sign of two methods delegating to each other rather than executing a terminating operation.
- The direct reference to self.append indicates you are invoking the instance method, not the superclass/list implementation, so any overridden append will be used.
