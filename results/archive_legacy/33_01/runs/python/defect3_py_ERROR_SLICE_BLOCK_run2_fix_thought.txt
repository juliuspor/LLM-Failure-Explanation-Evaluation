The bug arises from incorrect advancement of pos when translators consume codepoints. Currently after consumed!=0 they loop for pt in range(consumed): pos += char_count(code_point_at(code_units, pos)). This can raise IndexError because pos may reach length. Better to compute advance by reading codepoint_at at pos and increment by char_count for each consumed, but ensure not to call code_point_at beyond bounds. Instead, we can iterate consumed times and each time check pos < length then get cp=code_point_at(code_units,pos) and advance by char_count(cp). If pos is at end, break. Also simplify overall loop: when consumed==0 use code_point_at which is guarded. Ensure no IndexError by checking pos<length before calling code_point_at. Implement accordingly.
