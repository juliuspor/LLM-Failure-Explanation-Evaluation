The bug is due to translate advancing pos incorrectly when translators consume multiple codepoints. The original loop increments pos by char_count(code_point_at(code_units, pos)) inside a for loop but calling code_point_at after pos increment can go out of bounds. Fix by computing the codepoint at current pos before the loop iteration and then increment pos by its char count for each consumed. Also ensure that if consumed > 0 we advance by sum of char_count of each consumed codepoint, reading sequentially without re-reading beyond bounds.
