The bug is in translate(): when a translator consumes codepoints, the loop advancing pos used char_count(code_point_at(code_units, pos)) inside a for loop iterating consumed times but recalculates code_point_at with same pos leading to incorrect advancement when consumed>1. Should compute codepoint at current pos once each iteration and advance by its char_count, or better: for _ in range(consumed): cp=code_point_at(code_units,pos); pos+=char_count(cp). Also ensure consumed represents codepoints not code units; keep behavior. Implement fixed translate method accordingly.
