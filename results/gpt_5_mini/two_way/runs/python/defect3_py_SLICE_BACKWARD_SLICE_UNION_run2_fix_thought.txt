The bug stems from translate() advancing pos incorrectly when a translator consumes codepoints. translate_codepoint returns count of codepoints consumed (in codepoints, not UTF-16 units), but loop was using char_count on code_point_at at current pos inside a for loop causing potential wrong indexing. Simplify: when consumed>0, advance pos by sum of UTF-16 units of each consumed codepoint by iterating consumed times computing char_count of code_point_at at current pos before incrementing. Also avoid re-evaluating pos incorrectly. Implement a while loop to advance consumed times computing char_count at current pos.
