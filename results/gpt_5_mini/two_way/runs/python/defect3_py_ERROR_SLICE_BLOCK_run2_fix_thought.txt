The IndexError arises when translate_codepoint callers pass an index equal to length (out of range). The translate method should ensure it doesn't call translate_codepoint with pos >= length. The bug is likely that after consuming codepoints the loop advances pos incorrectly. Fix: in translate(), ensure translate_codepoint is called only if pos < length, and when consumed==0 advance by char_count of current codepoint. Also adjust post-consumed increment: instead of looping and calling code_point_at with same pos repeatedly (which may be out of range), compute increments using consumed and char_count on codepoints starting at original pos, but avoid calling code_point_at beyond bounds. Implement safer advancement by iterating consumed times while pos < length and advancing by char_count(code_point_at(code_units, pos)).
