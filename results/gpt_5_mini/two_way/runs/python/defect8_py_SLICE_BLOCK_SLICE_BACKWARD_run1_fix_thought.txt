The bug was caused by accessing locale_str[3] and [4] even when length != 5. I will restructure the conditions: validate length cases explicitly. For length==2 return language, for length==5 validate separator at 2 and uppercase country letters at 3-4 then return Locale; for length>=7 validate separators at 2 and 5 and country at 3-4, then return Locale with variant. This prevents out-of-range indexing.
