When create_number strips a type qualifier (last char), the remaining numeric string may become empty (e.g. input "l"). Accessing characters like numeric[0] causes IndexError. Fix by validating that the numeric or mant/exp/mantissa parts are non-empty after removing qualifiers and raise ValueError for invalid numbers instead of letting IndexError occur. Add checks where numeric or other substrings are used with char_at or is_digits to ensure length>0 and handle accordingly.
