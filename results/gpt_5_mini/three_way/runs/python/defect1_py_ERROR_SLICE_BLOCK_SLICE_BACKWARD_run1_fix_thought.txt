Instead handle provided minutes_offset possibly negative by ensuring its absolute value within 0..59 and applying sign of hours_offset (or provided minutes_offset) appropriately. Better: determine sign = 1 if hours_offset>=0 else -1; total_minutes = hours_offset*60 + sign*abs(minutes_offset). This preserves intent: minutes component is always positive magnitude.
