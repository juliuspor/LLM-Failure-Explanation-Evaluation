The bug diagnosis incorrectly identifies the issue in to_utf16_code_units (which correctly populates code_units). The real bug is in translate method: after translator consumes codepoints, the loop `for pt in range(consumed): pos += char_count(code_point_at(code_units, pos))` uses the wrong `pos` - it advances from the current pos each iteration instead of from where the previous iteration left off, causing incorrect positioning and infinite loops or skips. Fix by tracking advanced position incrementally.
