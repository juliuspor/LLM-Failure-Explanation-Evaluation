The validation `minutes_offset < 0 or minutes_offset > 59` incorrectly rejects all negative minutes regardless of hours_offset sign. Negative minutes are valid when hours_offset is negative (e.g., -2h -15m = -2:15). Fix by allowing negative minutes only when hours_offset is negative, and adjust the calculation logic to properly handle the total minutes: always compute total_minutes = hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset.
