The length check `if length != 2 and length != 5 and length < 7` incorrectly allows length=6 (rejected by <7 but passes !=2,!=5), but for length=7+ malformed strings can reach index accesses. Fix by changing to `if length < 2 or (length != 2 and length != 5 and length < 7)` to reject invalid lengths upfront. Also add upper length limit and validate variant length/format to prevent malformed strings from causing IndexError on slices.
