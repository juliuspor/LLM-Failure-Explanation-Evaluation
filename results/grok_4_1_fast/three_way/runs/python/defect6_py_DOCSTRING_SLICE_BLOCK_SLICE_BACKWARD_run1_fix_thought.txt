The infinite loop occurs because the while loop condition checks `mantissa % 10 == 0` but continues dividing even when mantissa becomes 1 (1 % 10 != 0 should exit). However, for very large integers, integer overflow or precision loss in the check_val computation `(mantissa // 10) * (10 ** (exp + 1))` can cause unexpected equality with value, preventing exit. Fix by adding `and mantissa > 1` to the while condition to safely exit when mantissa is too small to divide further.
