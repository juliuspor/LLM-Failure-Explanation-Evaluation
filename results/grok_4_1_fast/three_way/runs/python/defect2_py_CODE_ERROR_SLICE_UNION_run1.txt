When `value` is outside the bounds (e.g., less than `self._lower_bound` or greater than `self._upper_bound`), the normalized fraction `(value - self._lower_bound) / (self._upper_bound - self._lower_bound)` falls outside `[0, 1]`, making `g` fall outside `[0, 255]`. This triggers the `if g < 0 or g > 255` check on line 77, raising the ValueError.
