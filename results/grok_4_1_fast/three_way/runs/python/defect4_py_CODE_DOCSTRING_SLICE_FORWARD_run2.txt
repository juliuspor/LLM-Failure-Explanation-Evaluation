The code has a bug in the block updating `self._max_middle_index`. On the line where `max_middle_period` is fetched, it incorrectly uses `self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index)` instead of `self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index)`. This retrieves the period for the minimum middle index instead of the maximum middle index, causing `max_middle` to compare against the wrong value. As a result, the maximum middle index will not be updated correctly, and `self._max_middle_index` may point to the wrong item or fail to update when a larger middle timestamp is encountered.
