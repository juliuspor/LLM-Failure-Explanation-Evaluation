The clamping sets v = max(value, self._lower_bound) then v = min(v, self._upper_bound), but since both bounds are equal, v becomes that value regardless of input. However, the subsequent g calculation still divides by zero because it uses the original unclamped value and the zero denominator. The range check on lines 77-78 never executes due to this exception.
