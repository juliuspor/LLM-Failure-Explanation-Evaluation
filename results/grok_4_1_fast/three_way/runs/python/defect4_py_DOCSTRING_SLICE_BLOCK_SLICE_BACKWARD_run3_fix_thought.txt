The bug is caused by unconditional assignments to the min/max indices in the `else` branches of the `_update_bounds` method. These assignments (lines ~352, ~364, etc.) always set the indices to the new `index` even when the new period doesn't actually improve the min/max, causing the indices to always point to the most recently added item. The fix removes these unconditional assignments so indices are only updated when the new period actually sets a new min/max.
