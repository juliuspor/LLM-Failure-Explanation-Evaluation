The bug is in the incorrect handling of negative hours with positive minutes. For negative hours, `minutes_offset = hours_in_minutes - minutes_offset` wrongly subtracts positive minutes, making the total more negative (-120 - 15 = -135) instead of less negative (-120 + 15 = -105). The fix is to always add the absolute minutes contribution: compute total minutes as `abs(hours_offset) * 60 + minutes_offset`, apply the sign from hours_offset, then convert to millis.
