The bug is in the negative hours case where `minutes_offset = hours_in_minutes - minutes_offset` subtracts positive minutes from negative hours_in_minutes, making the total more negative (-120 - 15 = -135). For negative hours, minutes should be subtracted from the absolute value of hours_in_minutes (equivalent to adding the minutes magnitude to the negative total). Fix by always computing total_minutes = hours * 60 + minutes, then applying safe_multiply.
