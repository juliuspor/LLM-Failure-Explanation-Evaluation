The bug diagnosis is incorrect - `to_utf16_code_units` correctly populates `code_units` with a loop over each character in `s`. The real bug in `translate` is in the loop after `translate_codepoint` succeeds: `pos` is incremented using `char_count(code_point_at(code_units, pos))` repeatedly from the same `pos` without updating `pos` inside the loop, causing infinite loop or incorrect advancement. Fix by incrementing `pos` by `char_count` once per consumed codepoint.
