Since hours_in_minutes is negative and minutes_offset is positive (0-59 after validation on line 377), this produces a large negative value (e.g., hours_offset=-1, minutes_offset=30 gives -60 - 30 = -90). This negative minutes_offset then causes FieldUtils.safe_multiply on line 386 to throw ArithmeticError, which is caught and re-raised as ValueError('Offset is too large') on line 388.
