The bug is caused by incorrect indentation in the type inference logic. When both `array` and `element` are None, `inferred_type` is not set in the if/elif chain and the `inferred_type = object` line (which should be the else clause) executes regardless, causing a TypeError when `expected_type` is provided. Fixed by properly indenting `inferred_type = object` as the else clause of the if/elif chain.
