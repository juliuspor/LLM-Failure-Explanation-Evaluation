The bug occurs because invalid single-character inputs like 'l', 'F', 'junk' enter the non-digit last_char branch but don't match any type qualifiers ('lL', 'fFdD'), skipping the raise ValueError at the end of that block due to improper fall-through logic. The fix adds an early check after handling known qualifiers to raise ValueError for any unrecognized last_char, ensuring invalid inputs fail as expected.
