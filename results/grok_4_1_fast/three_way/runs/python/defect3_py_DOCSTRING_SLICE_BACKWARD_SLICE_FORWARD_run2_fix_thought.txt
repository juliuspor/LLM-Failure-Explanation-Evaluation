The bug diagnosis incorrectly identifies the issue. `to_utf16_code_units` correctly populates the list. The real bug is in `translate`: after `consumed > 0`, the loop `for pt in range(consumed): pos += char_count(code_point_at(code_units, pos))` calls `code_point_at(code_units, pos)` with the CURRENT `pos` (already advanced), causing double-skipping and IndexError. Fix by calculating codepoints upfront and advancing `pos` by total UTF-16 units consumed.
