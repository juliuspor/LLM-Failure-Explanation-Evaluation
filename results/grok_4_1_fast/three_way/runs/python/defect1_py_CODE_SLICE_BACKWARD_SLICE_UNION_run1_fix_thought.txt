The bug is in the incorrect sign handling for negative hours_offset. Instead of conditionally subtracting/adding minutes_offset based on the sign of hours_in_minutes, always add the absolute contributions: total_minutes = hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset. This correctly handles both positive and negative offsets (e.g., -1 hour -30 min = -90 min total). Remove the problematic conditional logic and simplify the calculation.
