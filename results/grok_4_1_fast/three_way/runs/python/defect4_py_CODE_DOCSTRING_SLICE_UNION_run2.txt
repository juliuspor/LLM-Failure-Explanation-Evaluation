The code has a bug in the `_max_middle_index` block. On the line where `max_middle_period` is fetched, it incorrectly uses `self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index)` instead of `self.get_data_item(self._max_middle_index)`. This means it retrieves the period from the minimum middle index item, not the maximum one. As a result, `max_middle` is calculated from the wrong period, causing incorrect comparisons and updates to `self._max_middle_index`. This leads to wrong tracking of the maximum middle timestamp index across the series.
