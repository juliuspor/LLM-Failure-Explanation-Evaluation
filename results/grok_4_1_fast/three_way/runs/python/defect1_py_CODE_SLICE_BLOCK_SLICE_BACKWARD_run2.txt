Later, 'offset = FieldUtils.safe_multiply(minutes_offset, DateTimeConstants.MILLIS_PER_MINUTE)' on line 387 passes this negative value, but safe_multiply likely throws ArithmeticError for values outside safe integer range or for negative offsets not representable as positive millis.
