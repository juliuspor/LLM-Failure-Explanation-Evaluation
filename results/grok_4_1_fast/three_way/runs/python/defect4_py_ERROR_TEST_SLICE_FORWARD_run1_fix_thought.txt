The bug is in the max_middle_index calculation block where `self._max_middle_index` is incorrectly used instead of `self._max_middle_index` when retrieving the period for comparison. This causes it to always compare against the min_middle period instead of the current max_middle period, leading to incorrect max tracking. Fixed by changing `self._min_middle_index` to `self._max_middle_index` in that block.
