The test `test_lang457_bad_inputs_raise_value_error` fails because `NumberUtils.create_number(s)` returns `s[index]` (line 35 in the implementation) instead of raising `ValueError` for invalid inputs like "l", "L", "f", "F", "junk", "bobL".
