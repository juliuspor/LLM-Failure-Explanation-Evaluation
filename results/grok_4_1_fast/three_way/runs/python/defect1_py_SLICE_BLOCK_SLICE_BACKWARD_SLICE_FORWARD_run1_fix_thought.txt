The bug occurs because `minutes_offset` is reassigned incorrectly when `hours_in_minutes` is negative, allowing it to exceed 59 (e.g., 120 - (-10) = 130). The fix computes total minutes as `hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset` directly, then validates the total before multiplying by `MILLIS_PER_MINUTE`. This ensures the final offset stays within bounds and eliminates the problematic reassignment.
