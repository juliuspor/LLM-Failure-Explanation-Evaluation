The bug is in the offset calculation logic for negative hours. When hours_offset is negative (e.g., -2), `hours_in_minutes` becomes negative (-120). The condition `if hours_in_minutes < 0: minutes_offset = hours_in_minutes - minutes_offset` incorrectly subtracts the positive minutes_offset (15), resulting in -135, which fails the range check. The fix normalizes the total offset by always adding the absolute hour minutes to the minutes_offset, then applying the sign via safe_multiply.
