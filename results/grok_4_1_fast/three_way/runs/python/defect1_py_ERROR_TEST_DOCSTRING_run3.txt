The code fails in the `test_negative_hours_negative_minutes` test when calling `DateTimeZone.for_offset_hours_minutes(-2, -15)`. The implementation raises `ValueError: Minutes out of range: -15` because it incorrectly validates the `minutes_offset` parameter as being out of range. According to the docstring and Joda-Time behavior, minutes_offset should be accepted in the range -59 to +59. For negative hours like -2, both positive minutes (15) and negative minutes (-15) should produce the offset -02:15 by treating minutes as extending the magnitude (total offset = hours * 3600000 + minutes * 60000 = -7200000 - 900000 = -8100000 ms). The validation logic wrongly rejects negative minutes when hours are negative instead of just computing the total offset and checking the final result's range.
