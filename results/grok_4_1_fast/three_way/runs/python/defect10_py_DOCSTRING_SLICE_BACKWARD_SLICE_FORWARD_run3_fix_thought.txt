The bug diagnosis is incorrect - lines 33-34 and 35 are the valid `char_at` utility function, not misplaced code. The actual bug is in `create_number`: when parsing non-digit suffixed numbers with exponents, `exp` is sliced to exclude the suffix (e.g., '1e3L' sets exp='3'), but later `all_zeros` checks use this truncated exp instead of the full exponent part including sign, causing incorrect zero detection for numbers like '-1e-0L'. Fixed by computing full exponent parts for `all_zeros` checks.
