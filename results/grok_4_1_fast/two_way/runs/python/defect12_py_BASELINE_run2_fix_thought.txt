The bug occurs because `lower` is not clamped to string length like `upper` is. When `lower > len(text)`, `StringUtils.index_of` with `start=lower` still works (clamps to 0), but the diagnosis indicates `substring_java` is called with invalid params due to unhandled `lower`. Clamp `lower` to `len(text)` early, matching `upper` logic, to prevent any out-of-bounds issues.
