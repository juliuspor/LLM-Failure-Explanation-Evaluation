The bug is in the conditional logic for negative hours. When hours are negative, the code incorrectly does `hours_in_minutes - minutes_offset` which subtracts a negative minutes_offset (making it positive), canceling the minutes contribution. The fix is to always compute total minutes as `hours_offset * 60 + minutes_offset` regardless of sign, then convert to millis. This correctly handles -2 hours -15 minutes as -135 total minutes.
