The bug is caused by unconditional assignment of `index` to min/max index variables when they are -1 (first item case). This is correct for the first item but the diagnosis highlights the pattern issue. The real bug is in the max_middle_index block at line ~370: `self.get_data_item(self._min_middle_index)` should be `self._max_middle_index`. This causes incorrect comparison for max middle calculation, leading to wrong indices.
