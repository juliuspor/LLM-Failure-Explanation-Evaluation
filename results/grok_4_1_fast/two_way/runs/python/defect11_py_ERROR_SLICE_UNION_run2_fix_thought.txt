The bug occurs because `random_letters_numbers` passes `start=0, end=0` to `_random_internal`, which sets `gap=0` when `letters=True` or `numbers=True`, causing `_next_int_java(rnd, 0)` to raise ValueError('bound must be positive'). The fix sets proper ASCII bounds for letters/numbers: start=48 (0), end=123 (z+1), matching Java's behavior and avoiding zero gap.
