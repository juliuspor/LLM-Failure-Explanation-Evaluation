The infinite loop occurs because the loop body executes `mantissa //= 10` and `exp += 1` *after* the `if check_val == value:` check, but the diagnosis incorrectly states it exits early without modification. For large powers of 10, `10 ** (exp + 1)` overflows `int` to `float` and loses precision, causing `check_val != value` after a few iterations, but `mantissa % 10 == 0` remains true, trapping in an infinite loop. Fix by using a simple counter for trailing zeros with string conversion or math.log10 to avoid precision loss.
