The bug occurs because `random_letters_numbers` passes `start=0, end=0` to `random`, which sets `gap=0`. When `chars=None`, `_next_int_java(rnd, 0)` raises ValueError since bound=0 is not positive. The fix ensures a valid positive gap by setting proper defaults when start==end==0, matching the internal logic: ASCII printable for default, letters range for letters/numbers.
