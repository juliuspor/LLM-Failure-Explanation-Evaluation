The bug occurs because `random_letters_numbers` passes `start=0, end=0` to `_random_internal`, which sets `gap=0` and causes `_next_int_java(rnd, 0)` to raise ValueError. The fix explicitly handles the `letters/numbers` case in `random` by setting proper ASCII bounds (32-127) when both `start` and `end` are 0, matching the internal logic.
