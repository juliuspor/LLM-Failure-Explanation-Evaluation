The diagnosis incorrectly identifies the bug. `to_utf16_code_units` correctly populates the code_units list in `translate` method (line ~130). The real bug is in the main `translate` loop: after `translate_codepoint` returns `consumed > 0`, it incorrectly advances `pos` by summing `char_count(code_point_at(code_units, pos))` *consumed* times starting from the already-consumed position, causing double-skipping and missing characters. Fix by advancing `pos` directly by the returned `consumed` count.
