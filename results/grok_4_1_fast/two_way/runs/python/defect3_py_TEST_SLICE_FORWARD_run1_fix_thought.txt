The bug occurs because when translate_codepoint returns 0 for a surrogate pair, code_point_at correctly identifies the full codepoint but the position advancement uses len(c) which is 2, while pos is only incremented by 1 initially before the loop. For surrogate pairs, this leaves pos at the low surrogate index. The fix ensures pos advances by the full char_count(codepoint) after copying unhandled codepoints.
