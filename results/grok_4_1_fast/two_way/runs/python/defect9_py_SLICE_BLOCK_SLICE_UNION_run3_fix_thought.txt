The bug occurs because the length validation `len(search_list) != len(replacement_list)` fails to catch cases where `replacement_list` is shorter than `search_list`. The `search_length` uses `len(search_list)`, causing IndexError when accessing `replacement_list[i]` for `i >= len(replacement_list)`. Fix by using the minimum length of both lists for `search_length` to ensure safe indexing.
