The bug diagnosis incorrectly identifies the issue in to_utf16_code_units (which is correctly implemented). The real bug is in CharSequenceTranslator.translate: after converting input_seq to code_units = to_utf16_code_units(input_seq), the loop incorrectly advances pos by char_count(code_point_at(...)) which recomputes codepoints and can skip surrogate pairs or advance incorrectly. Fix by advancing pos by consumed code units directly.
