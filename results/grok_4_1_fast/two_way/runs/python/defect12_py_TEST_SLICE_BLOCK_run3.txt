The test `test_abbreviate_lower_beyond_length` fails because it calls `WordUtils.abbreviate("0123456789", 15, 20, None)`. The input string has length 10. Here, `start=15` and `end=20`, both greater than `len(s)=10`, so `start > len(s)` and `end > len(s)` are true. This triggers `raise IndexError(f"String index out of range: {end}")`, causing the test to fail instead of returning "0123456789" as expected.
