The ValueError occurs because `gap = end - start` can be 0 when both `letters` and `numbers` are False and `chars` is None, making `start=0` and `end=127` but the surrogate/private surrogate filtering rejects all characters, causing infinite loop until `_next_int_java(rnd, 0)` is called. Fix by adding explicit check for `gap <= 0` before the loop and raising appropriate error.
