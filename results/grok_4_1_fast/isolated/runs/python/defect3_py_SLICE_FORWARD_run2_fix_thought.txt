The bug diagnosis refers to a different line numbering. The actual issue is in CsvEscaper.translate_codepoint(): after detecting and writing a surrogate pair (lines 38-43), it returns 2 (codepoints consumed) but the translate() method incorrectly advances pos by summing char_count() for each of 2 codepoints, causing pos to advance by 4 instead of 2, leading to skipping or incorrect positioning. Fix by advancing pos by the raw number of code units consumed.
