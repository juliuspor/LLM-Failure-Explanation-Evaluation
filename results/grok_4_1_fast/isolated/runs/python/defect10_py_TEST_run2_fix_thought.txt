The bug occurs because single-letter inputs like 'l', 'L', 'f', 'F' pass the early checks and enter the non-digit last_char block, but the validation conditions for these suffixes (requiring digits before the suffix) fail silently without raising ValueError. Added explicit raise ValueError at the start of the non-digit last_char block for invalid cases, ensuring bad inputs like single letters or 'bobL' raise ValueError as expected by the test.
