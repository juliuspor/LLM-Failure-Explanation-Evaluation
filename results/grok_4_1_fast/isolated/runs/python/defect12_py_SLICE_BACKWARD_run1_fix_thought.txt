The bug is in `substring_java` called by `abbreviate`. It incorrectly raises IndexError when `end > len(s)`, but Python slicing clamps `end` to `len(s)` gracefully. Fix by changing `end > len(s)` to `end > len(s) + 1` to match Python's behavior where `end == len(s) + 1` is allowed (clamps to end of string). `abbreviate` logic is correct.
