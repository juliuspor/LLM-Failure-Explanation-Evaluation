Consent

Criteria are the specific rules you will use to judge each explanation (you’ll see the criteria definitions on each task page).

In this study you will judge software failure explanations across 8 defects. Before the first defect, you will complete a short warm-up task to get familiar with the criteria and the interface. For each defect, you will:

  1. Label 3 explanations (A/B/C) on 4 criteria (C2/C3/C4/C6) (binary).
  2. Rate how difficult/uncertain judging was for each criterion (1–5).
