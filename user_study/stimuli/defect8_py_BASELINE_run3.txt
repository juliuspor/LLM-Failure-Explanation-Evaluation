Why it fails (root cause)
- The method assumes that when there is a third character after the first underscore (position 3) there must always be a two-letter uppercase country code. It does not handle the valid case language__variant (an empty country), where you have two underscores in a row and the variant begins after the second underscore.
- In other words, the check at lines 237–240 is too strict: it treats the '_' at position 3 (the empty country marker) as invalid rather than recognizing the empty country + variant form.

3. Otherwise (locale_str[3] != '_'), validate the two-letter uppercase country at locale_str[3] and [4] as the existing code does, and continue to handle the optional variant at index 6+.
